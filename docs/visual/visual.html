Visual Style


Make your service look and feel like Osirium. Many styles are already established within the Osirium products and components and pages will not need any additional styling. If you need to apply styles manually, you should still follow existing Osirium conventions. For example, do not assign new meanings to colours, do not change the style of buttons or adjust the thickness of borders on form.
